About Sunbirds

Sunbirds is built around a simple idea: the best trips shouldn't have to end at the campground gate.

Set under wide skies and easy mornings, we're creating a community where RVs, park models, and tiny homes share the same warm horizon — full-service sites, resort comforts, and a village that feels like it was always here.

Whether you're chasing the season, parking for a spell, or planting roots, you'll find shaded sites, thoughtful amenities, and neighbors who wave. Think of it less as a lot to park on and more as a place to belong to.

We're putting the finishing touches on everything now. Add your email above and we'll bring you along from the ground up — first reservations, opening celebrations, and the small updates in between.
